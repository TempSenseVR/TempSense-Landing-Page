About EyeTrackVR

Pioneering Open and Modular Eye Tracking

Our Misson

EyeTrackVR emerged from a simple idea: eye tracking should be accessible to everyone. Since then, we have strived to lower the barrier to entry of eye tracking in virtual reality.

The Journey

Starting as a simple winter break project in 2021, we've grown into a global community of thousands. Through constant iteration and relentless R&D, we've expanded both our capabilities and accessibility.
